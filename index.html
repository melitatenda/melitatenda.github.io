Research
Interests

Three questions drive my work: how can AI serve society, how should it be governed, and how do humans and machines share a world?

1

AI for Social Good

How artificial intelligence can be applied to improve societal outcomes: making public services more accessible, enhancing healthcare delivery, and improving educational tools. I am particularly curious about ways AI can address systemic inefficiencies and contribute to measurable social impact.

2

Policy & Governance for Emerging Technologies

How policy, governance, and organizational frameworks can guide the safe and responsible deployment of advanced technologies, especially AI compliance and governance, and how developing countries can help tech startups deploy AI models ethically and transparently.

3

Robotics & Society

The societal implications of robotics and autonomous systems: how robots can interact with people safely, ethically, and effectively. I am particularly curious about human-robot interaction and designing robots that are socially aware as well as technically capable.
